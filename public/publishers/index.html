Publishers

    Getting Started

    The Coull Delivery Platform enables you to deliver a selection of effective and unique ad formats across the highest quality video inventory. Coull delivers ads via the IAB’s VAST/VPAID schema, which provides support for a number of different formats over video, including: Linear pre-roll, mid-roll and post-roll MP4 or FLV video ads Non-linear GIF/JPG/PNG, Flash (SWF), text, HTML, <iframe> and <script> overlay banners Interactive Flash and HTML5 VPAID ads
